StarCraft 2

If you are wondering what StarCraft 2 is. StarCraft 2 is an RTS(Real-time strategy) game. The main idea is to destroy your opponent. you do that by moving units and building structures to overcome your opponent. But that's just the basic premise of the game. There's a lot more to Starcraft 2 than just destroying your opponent. like how to manage your resources, moving units, the counter to certain units, all the strategies in the game. These are some of the few things you need to know to play Starcraft 2. This tutorial expects you to understand the basic knowledge of Starcraft 2. If you haven't played the tutorial, here's a good video to watch to understand how the game works.

The start of the tutorial

  1. When you load into your game you will be able to see what race your opponent is playing. You'll be able to tell because the enemy player will have a banner with a picture of their race.

  3. what's assumed the opponent is going for a ground Army and with weak anti-air.

  4. knowing the weakness of your opponent. You should start building infrastructure. so you can wager War. So you will want to secure your lower expansion And you probably won't want 4 vespene gas geysers.

  5. Now you will want to construct a gateway to spawn Basic Ground units. These ground units will be used in defense of your base.

  6. now send your defense units to your lower expansion. The opponent will have to destroy your lower expansion to get your main base.

  8. By the 12 minute mark. you should have 10 Cruisers or so and some units you've been using to defend your base. to make an assault.

  9. In your case, this will be a defining moment if you win the game or lose it. by now your resources have run out on both of your expansions. so this will be your final Army. To win the game pretty much.

  10. This will be the final step. All you do now. is Click F2 then press A and click on your opponent base. That should get you the win.
