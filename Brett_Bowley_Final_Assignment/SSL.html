What is the lock icon by the website URL in my browser?

That lock means that the website has secure encryption on your information. The name of the lock is SSL. You may be asking what SSL is and what does it stand for? That is an abbreviation for Secure Sockets Layer. I will be using the shorthand SSL.

If you're wondering if you're securely browsing the internet when you go on random websites and you don't know if they're secure, there's a handy way to figure this out.

If that lock icon appears locked, the website is secure. Why is that good for you? You want to keep your information safe and out of the wrong people's hands. Protecting your credit card is important so you don't have people spending all your money on it.
